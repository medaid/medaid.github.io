About

Welcome to medaid! Glad you could make it...

Let me start by saying that this website is by no means an authority on medical charities. The statistics used are not guaranteed to be correct, and have been pulled from various (somewhat credible) sources from around the internet. Having said that, it is crucial that users report incorrect information as soon as possible by clicking the little flag beside the charity in question. I am only one person, and I can't ensure the viability of this information without help. I created medaid in the hopes that it would allow people to discover medical or health related charities that they have never heard of before. Hopefully this will lead to a few more donations for these charities in the future.

I would also like to point out that my rating system is entirely arbitrary, and only comes from my own personal knowledge of the charity, and a quick google search. I encourage visitors to research the credibility of these charities on their own time, and make an informed decision before donating or supporting them in any way.

Medaid is constantly undergoing changes and improvements, so don't be suprised if it looks drastically different the next time you see it.

Finally, if you like medaid, you might want to check out my blog/personal site (steamer360.github.io) for more projects like this. Also follow me on social media (if you want).
